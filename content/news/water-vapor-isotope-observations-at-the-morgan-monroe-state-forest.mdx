![MMSF Lab](){: .align-left} During July 5-Sep 5, 2008, Lixin Wang worked at Morgan-Monroe State Forest Ameriflux tower site (Indiana University). He collaborated with Danilo Dragoni to test the performance of the Water Vapor Isotope Analyzer (Los Gatos Research) and to couple the continuous isotope measurements with eddy covariance tower.
