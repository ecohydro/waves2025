![Trenton Augering](){: .align-left} Our group has received support from an award from the Office of International Science and Engineering at the National Science Foundation. The award is in the amount of $15,000 for Trenton Franz's Doctoral Dissertation Enhancement project “U.S. Kenya DDEP: The impact of macropores on the spatial and temporal patterns of soil moisture in dryland ecosystems of central Kenya.” 

**Congratulations Trenton!**
