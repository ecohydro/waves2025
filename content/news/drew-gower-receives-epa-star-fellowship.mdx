[Drew Gower]() just received a <a href="http://epa.gov/ncer/fellow/" target="_blank">Science to Achieve Results (STAR) Fellowship for Graduate Environmental Study</a> awarded by the <a href="http://www.epa.gov/" target="_blank">Environmental Protection Agency</a>. <!--more-->The fellowship will cover his stipend and a portion of his tuition for two years, starting in September. Drew's proposal focused on improving water resource management among irrigators in the Laikipia Region of Kenya.

Congratulations Drew!
