[Lixin Wang](), former member of the EcoHydrology Lab, was recently interview to discuss his work on the effects of drought on global production of legumes. <!--more-->According to Lixin: "Our results could facilitate the development and selection of existing legume species, as well as better management for drought-prone regions by testing whether these species become more or less sensitive to climate variations, particularly drought."
