[UAV image]() Eric Principato is starting to work in the Caylor Lab as the lab mechanical engineer for [Lyndon Estes](). <!--more-->  Eric did his first copter flight training this weekend and picked up piloting quickly, going from zero experience to flying a data collection mission all with zero crashes and no broken copter components in only two days.

<!--more-->

Eric did a flight over Herbert Run to collect photos for use in learning Photoscan.
